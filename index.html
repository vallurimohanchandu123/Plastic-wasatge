Plastic Pollution

Plastic pollution is a global problem. Every year 19-23 million tonnes of plastic waste leaks into aquatic ecosystems, polluting lakes, rivers and seas


The Importance of the Plastic Waste Problem

Environmental Impact

The plastic waste problem has severe consequences for our planet. Plastic pollution contributes to habitat destruction, soil pollution, and air contamination.

Human Health Risks

Plastic waste pollutes our food and water, causing serious health risks. Over time, this pollution can lead to respiratory problems, heart disease, and cancer.

Climate Crisis

Plastic production and incineration contribute to greenhouse gas emissions, exacerbating the climate crisis. The plastic waste problem is a threat to all life on earth
