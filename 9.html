Current Statistics on Plastic Wastage








The Scope of the Problem



Every year, we produce over 300 million tons of plastic, 50% of which is for single-use purposes. Only 9% of all plastic ever produced has been recycled. More than 8 million tons of plastic waste end up in the world's oceans every year.

The Cost of Inaction

Plastic pollution costs the world billions of dollars in health, environmental, and economic damages. By 2050, it is projected that there will be more plastic in the oceans than fish.

The Power of Consumer Choices

Consumers have the power to drive change by supporting sustainable brands and reducing plastic consumption. By making conscious choices, we can protect our planet and hold corporations accountable.

Advocacy and Involvement in Plastic Waste Reduction



By advocating for policy change, educating others, and supporting sustainable brands, we can make a difference in the fight against plastic waste.
