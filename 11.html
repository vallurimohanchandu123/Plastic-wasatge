Causes of Plastic Wastage








Single-Use Plastics



Single-use plastics, such as straws, bags, and cups, are a significant contributor to plastic waste. They are discarded after just one use, adding to landfill and polluting our oceans.

Improper Disposal

When plastic waste is not disposed of correctly, it can take hundreds of years to decompose, generating greenhouse gases that contribute to climate change. This waste is also a threat to wildlife and ecosystems.

Lack of Awareness and Education

Many people are unaware of the harm caused by plastic waste and do not know how to properly recycle or dispose of plastic goods. Education and awareness campaigns are essential in reducing plastic waste.
